About TruthForge

An intelligent, multilingual, and explainable AI system designed to combat fake news with political context awareness.

Our Mission

In today's digital age, misinformation spreads faster than truth. TruthForge was built to empower users with transparent, explainable AI that doesn't just say "fake" or "real" — it explains why.

Our system supports multiple languages including Bengali, English, Hindi, Arabic, Spanish, and French, making it truly accessible across borders.

Thesis Project

This is a final year undergraduate thesis project focused on developing a robust fake news detection system using Explainable AI (XAI) and incorporating political context for better accuracy.

Key Innovations

  • → Explainable AI with word-level highlights
  • → Political Bias & Leaning Detection
  • → Image + Text Analysis
